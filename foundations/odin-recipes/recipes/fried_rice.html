Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Additional dish of choice

Cooking Instructions

  1. Leave cooked rice in the freezer for one day
  2. Take out leftover chilled rice and steam the rice
  3. Add the egg into the pan and fry until welldone, recipe here
  4. Cook dish of choice until well done
  5. pour rice into the pan and add the egg and dish of choice to saute in the pan
  6. Plate the rice
