Steam Fish Recipe

Ingredients

  • fish
  • soy sauce
  • water
  • ginger (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. wash fish properly and take out all its organs
  2. plate the fish on a silver platter
  3. add some water and soysauce to the mix
  4. Blend ginger and spread it around the fish to bring out the aroma
  5. pour water into the pan and add a stand to put the platter and steam the fish
  6. Leave for 1 hour and take out the platter, fish is ready to be served
